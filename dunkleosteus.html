Dunkleosteus-Amphithere Hybrid


This model was inspired by the dunkleosteus. I was interested in making a more complex model with more textural components to train my sculpting skills. I thought it would be interesting to create an amphithere with dunkleosteus features.

The model is still currently a work in progress as I hope to retoplogize, texture paint, and rig the model. Below are the PureRef board I used alongside the model sheet I drew, a blockout of the model, and a 360 render of the sculpt.

Game Development Club Final Project

Game Link: https://avy-gagneja.itch.io/2d-game-design-project-2024

This game was our final project for the BASIS Oro Valley Game Development club.

I was responsible for founding and leading a club with my friend, teaching 30 6th-12th graders to program and make art for games.

For our cumulative final project, the students in the club were responsible for making all the pixel art and programming the mechanics of a basic 2D platformer. My friend and I were responsible for making lesson plans and create a basic framework for the students to program off of.

Defeat all the enemies and squat on the toilet of the boss to win!
